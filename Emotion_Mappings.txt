    HAPPY (Tier 1)
Description: Happy is a positive emotional state characterized by feelings of pleasure, contentment, and wellbeing. It encompasses a wide range of pleasant experiences from quiet satisfaction to intense joy.
Common Triggers:

Achieving goals or success
Spending time with loved ones
Engaging in enjoyable activities
Receiving good news
Experiencing beauty or pleasure
Meeting personal needs and desires

Characteristics:

Increased energy and motivation
Positive outlook on life
Desire to connect and share with others
Smiling, laughing, open body language
Mental clarity and creativity
Feeling light or energized

Physical Sensations:

Warmth in chest or throughout body
Relaxed muscles
Smiling (sometimes involuntary)
Elevated or steady comfortable heart rate
Feeling of lightness or buoyancy
Increased energy levels


Tier 2 Emotions Under Happy
1. JOYFUL (Tier 2)
Description: Joyful is an intense, high-energy form of happiness characterized by exuberance, excitement, and delight. It's the feeling of being overflowing with positive emotion and wanting to express it outwardly.
Common Triggers:

Receiving exciting news
Reuniting with loved ones
Experiencing something thrilling or delightful
Celebrating achievements or milestones
Unexpected pleasant surprises
Being in love or experiencing deep connection

Characteristics:

High energy and enthusiasm
Expressive and outward-focused
Desire to move, dance, or celebrate
Laughing frequently
Sharing excitement with others
Feeling fully alive and present
Expansive, open gestures

Physical Sensations:

Bubbling or bursting feeling in chest
Urge to jump, dance, or move
Wide smiles and bright eyes
Rapid, excited heartbeat
Tingling sensations
Feeling like you could "burst" with happiness
Excess energy throughout body


Tier 3 Under Joyful:
ECSTATIC

Description: An overwhelming, almost transcendent state of extreme joy and rapture. The peak of positive emotional experience.
Triggers: Life-changing good news, spiritual experiences, profound moments of connection, achieving a major life goal
Characteristics: Feeling like time stops, inability to contain excitement, tears of joy, shouting or expressing loudly, feeling invincible
Physical: Racing heart, full-body chills, trembling with excitement, feeling faint or dizzy from emotion

ENTHUSIASTIC

Description: Intense eagerness, excitement, and energy directed toward something you care about or find meaningful.
Triggers: Starting a new project, discovering a new passion, engaging in favorite activities, anticipating something exciting
Characteristics: High motivation, eagerness to participate or start, animated speech and gestures, infectious energy, talking rapidly about interests
Physical: Restless energy, leaning forward, rapid movements, difficulty sitting still, elevated heart rate

ELATED

Description: A soaring feeling of triumph and exhilaration, often following success or relief from worry.
Triggers: Passing an important test, receiving recognition, overcoming obstacles, hearing awaited good news
Characteristics: Feeling "on top of the world," pride combined with joy, desire to celebrate, sense of vindication, relief mixed with happiness
Physical: Chest feels expanded, arms raised or reaching upward, jumping or bouncing, feeling lighter than air

FREE / LIBERATED

Description: A sense of release from constraints, feeling unbound and able to fully express yourself without limitation.
Triggers: Finishing major obligations, leaving toxic situations, making empowering decisions, experiencing wide-open spaces, travel
Characteristics: Spontaneity, feeling unburdened, taking deep breaths, desire for movement or adventure, shedding restrictions, authentic self-expression
Physical: Deep breathing, chest expansion, arms outstretched, running or spinning, feeling weightless, tension releasing from shoulders


2. PLAYFUL (Tier 2)
Description: Playful is a lighthearted, fun-loving emotional state characterized by humor, spontaneity, and not taking things too seriously. It's about finding amusement and joy in the moment without pressure or expectation.
Common Triggers:

Being with friends or children
Engaging in games or recreational activities
Finding humor in situations
Feeling safe and comfortable enough to let loose
Nostalgia for carefree times
Creative activities without stakes

Characteristics:

Teasing and joking around
Spontaneous laughter
Childlike wonder and silliness
Not taking yourself seriously
Living in the present moment
Willingness to look foolish
Creative, improvisational thinking

Physical Sensations:

Lightness in body and spirit
Giggles or frequent smiling
Bouncy, energetic movements
Relaxed muscles
Twinkling eyes
Urge to move playfully


Tier 3 Under Playful:
SILLY

Description: Embracing foolishness and absurdity in a joyful way, letting go of dignity for the sake of fun.
Triggers: Being with close friends, watching comedy, engaging in ridiculous activities, playing with children, feeling uninhibited
Characteristics: Making funny faces or voices, laughing at yourself, doing things for pure amusement, being deliberately ridiculous, not caring what others think
Physical: Uncontrolled giggles, doubled over laughing, floppy movements, exaggerated expressions

CHEEKY

Description: Playfully impudent or mischievous, enjoying gentle rule-bending or teasing in a fun-spirited way.
Triggers: Opportunities for harmless mischief, playful banter, getting away with something minor, teasing loved ones
Characteristics: Mischievous grin, witty comebacks, testing boundaries playfully, winking, gentle provocation, enjoying reactions
Physical: Smirk or sly smile, raised eyebrows, glint in the eye, suppressed laughter

GOOFY

Description: Awkward or clumsy in an endearing, humorous way; not trying to be cool or composed.
Triggers: Making mistakes that turn out funny, physical comedy, being comfortable enough to be awkward, trying new things without skill
Characteristics: Self-deprecating humor, embracing clumsiness, making exaggerated movements, being purposefully dorky, laughing at own mistakes
Physical: Clumsy movements, tripping over words, exaggerated gestures, wide goofy smile

MISCHIEVOUS

Description: Enjoying harmless trouble-making or playful scheming; the pleasure of planning something naughty but not harmful.
Triggers: Planning surprises, playing pranks, finding loopholes, playful defiance, getting away with harmless rule-bending
Characteristics: Secretive smiles, conspiratorial behavior, planning pranks, enjoying suspense, playful rebellion, testing limits
Physical: Rubbing hands together, sneaky movements, suppressed giggles, shifty eyes (playfully)


3. CONTENT (Tier 2)
Description: Content is a calm, satisfied form of happiness characterized by acceptance and comfort with the present moment. It's feeling that things are "good enough" and experiencing peace with what is.
Common Triggers:

Meeting basic needs comfortably
Completing tasks or goals
Quiet moments of reflection
Being in comfortable, familiar environments
Letting go of striving or wanting
Simple pleasures (warm tea, cozy blanket)

Characteristics:

Lack of desire to change anything
Present-moment awareness
Gentle smile or neutral pleasant expression
Ease and comfort with current state
Not needing more or different
Quiet satisfaction
Reduced mental chatter

Physical Sensations:

Relaxed, loose muscles
Slow, deep breathing
Comfortable posture (sitting back, leaning)
Soft, gentle expression
Warmth and comfort throughout body
Steady, calm heartbeat
Feeling settled


Tier 3 Under Content:
SATISFIED

Description: Feeling that your needs or desires have been met; a sense of fulfillment and completion.
Triggers: Finishing a good meal, completing work, achieving goals, having needs met, receiving what you wanted
Characteristics: Sense of completion, no lingering wants, pleasant closure, feeling "full" in a good way, readiness to rest
Physical: Sighing contentedly, sitting back, relaxed posture, full feeling (if food-related), muscle relaxation

COMFORTABLE

Description: Physical and emotional ease; feeling at home, safe, and without discomfort or stress.
Triggers: Being in familiar spaces, wearing cozy clothes, safe relationships, predictable routines, warm environments
Characteristics: Lack of tension, feeling "at home," ease of being yourself, no need to perform or impress, natural and unforced
Physical: Loose muscles, settling into furniture, slow movements, warm sensations, soft breathing

AT EASE

Description: Free from worry or concern; a state of mental and emotional relaxation and trust that things are okay.
Triggers: Resolving concerns, safe environments, trusted company, meditation or mindfulness, vacation time
Characteristics: Clear mind, lack of anxiety, trust in the moment, mental spaciousness, not monitoring for threats, gentle awareness
Physical: Dropped shoulders, unclenched jaw, soft eyes, slow breathing, open posture

FULFILLED

Description: A deep sense of completeness and meaning; feeling that your life has purpose and you're living aligned with your values.
Triggers: Meaningful work, helping others, creative expression, spiritual experiences, living authentically, long-term goal achievement
Characteristics: Sense of purpose, feeling complete, gratitude for life, lack of existential anxiety, inner peace, feeling "enough"
Physical: Fullness in chest (not pressure), grounded feeling, steady breathing, relaxed face, upright but relaxed posture


4. PEACEFUL (Tier 2)
Description: Peaceful is a tranquil, serene state of happiness characterized by inner calm, absence of conflict or disturbance, and harmony with your surroundings. It's a still, quiet form of wellbeing.
Common Triggers:

Nature experiences (forests, beaches, mountains)
Meditation or prayer
Resolution of conflicts
Quiet, undisturbed environments
End of stressful periods
Feeling safe and secure
Gentle, rhythmic activities (walking, breathing)

Characteristics:

Mental stillness and clarity
Absence of agitation or urgency
Feeling in harmony with surroundings
Soft, gentle presence
Acceptance of what is
Reduced mental activity
Sense of timelessness

Physical Sensations:

Very slow, deep breathing
Complete muscle relaxation
Soft, unfocused gaze
Still, quiet body
Slow, steady heartbeat
Feeling weightless or floating
Warmth or coolness that's pleasant


Tier 3 Under Peaceful:
CALM

Description: A state of mental and emotional tranquility; absence of anxiety, anger, or disturbance.
Triggers: Deep breathing, removing from chaos, problem resolution, soothing environments, reassurance, meditation
Characteristics: Clear thinking, emotional stability, unruffled by disturbances, steady responses, inner quietness, balanced perspective
Physical: Relaxed facial muscles, steady breathing, loose shoulders, stable posture, slow movements

SERENE

Description: A profound, almost ethereal state of peace and tranquility; undisturbed composure and clarity.
Triggers: Beautiful natural settings, spiritual experiences, deep meditation, moments of perfect stillness, sunrise/sunset
Characteristics: Feeling like still water, transcendent calm, beauty in simplicity, complete acceptance, unity with surroundings, effortless presence
Physical: Very still body, soft breathing almost imperceptible, relaxed face, slight smile, eyes gently closed or soft focus

TRANQUIL

Description: Free from disturbance or turmoil; a quiet, peaceful state of being undisturbed.
Triggers: Gentle environments, after stress has passed, quiet mornings, peaceful settings, solitude when desired
Characteristics: Unhurried, gentle awareness, no pressing concerns, comfort with silence, smooth emotional state, lack of internal conflict
Physical: Slow, steady movements, soft breathing, relaxed throughout, comfortable stillness, gentle posture

RELAXED

Description: Free from tension and anxiety; body and mind at ease without effort or strain.
Triggers: End of work, vacation, massage, bath, comfortable settings, removing stressors, exercise completion
Characteristics: Lack of urgency, mental ease, physical looseness, willingness to let things unfold, not holding on tightly, flowing rather than forcing
Physical: Loose limbs, dropped shoulders, unclenched jaw, deep breathing, sinking feeling, melting into surroundings


5. INTERESTED (Tier 2)
Description: Interested is an engaged, curious state of happiness characterized by wanting to learn, explore, or understand more. It's the pleasure of discovery and mental stimulation.
Common Triggers:

Encountering something new or novel
Learning opportunities
Mysteries or puzzles
Engaging conversations
Complex ideas or systems
Discovering new hobbies or interests
Questions without obvious answers

Characteristics:

Mental alertness and focus
Asking questions
Leaning in or moving toward stimulus
Desire to explore further
Absorption in subject matter
Active listening
Open-mindedness

Physical Sensations:

Eyes widening or brightening
Leaning forward
Raised eyebrows
Alert, attentive posture
Mental energy and clarity
Tingling of anticipation
Focused, narrow attention


Tier 3 Under Interested:
CURIOUS

Description: A strong desire to know or learn something; wondering and questioning with openness.
Triggers: Mysteries, new information, things that don't make sense, different perspectives, unexplored topics, "how" and "why" questions
Characteristics: Asking questions, seeking information, exploring possibilities, open to answers, non-judgmental inquiry, childlike wonder
Physical: Head tilted, eyes scanning, leaning in, touching or examining objects, opening up posture

INQUISITIVE

Description: Eager to investigate, probe, and understand deeply; actively seeking knowledge and understanding.
Triggers: Complex problems, research opportunities, detailed topics, interviewing others, investigating mysteries, deep topics
Characteristics: Persistent questioning, digging deeper, not satisfied with surface answers, analytical thinking, following threads of inquiry, detective-like
Physical: Focused gaze, furrowed brow (from concentration), leaning forward, note-taking posture, attentive stillness

FASCINATED

Description: Captured by intense interest and wonder; captivated or mesmerized by something compelling.
Triggers: Extraordinary phenomena, mastery in others, complex beauty, revelations, perfect explanations, witnessing excellence
Characteristics: Complete absorption, losing track of time, can't look away, sense of awe mixed with interest, mental expansion, "I never knew that" moments
Physical: Wide eyes, mouth slightly open, very still (absorbed), holding breath, riveted attention, goosebumps

ENGAGED

Description: Fully involved and absorbed in an activity or subject; actively participating with focus and energy.
Triggers: Meaningful work, stimulating conversation, challenging tasks, collaborative projects, games or puzzles, creative flow
Characteristics: Full attention devoted, losing self-consciousness, flow state, active participation, mental and physical involvement, time distortion
Physical: Active body language, animated expression, energized movements, focused eyes, quick responses, alert posture


6. PROUD (Tier 2)
Description: Proud is a sense of deep pleasure and satisfaction derived from achievements, qualities, or associations that reflect well on oneself. It combines happiness with self-respect and confidence.
Common Triggers:

Accomplishing difficult goals
Receiving recognition or praise
Overcoming obstacles
Others acknowledging your worth
Living up to your values
Mastering new skills
Others' achievements (if connected to them)

Characteristics:

Standing tall, upright posture
Sense of self-worth and validation
Desire to share achievements
Confidence in abilities
Validation of effort and struggle
Feeling deserving of success
Connection between effort and outcome

Physical Sensations:

Chest expanding or lifting
Shoulders back
Head held high
Feeling taller or bigger
Warmth in chest
Strong, grounded stance
Energy radiating outward


Tier 3 Under Proud:
ACCOMPLISHED

Description: Feeling that you have successfully completed something significant; the satisfaction of having achieved a goal.
Triggers: Finishing projects, reaching milestones, graduating, earning promotions, completing challenges, building something
Characteristics: Sense of completion, tangible results to point to, relief mixed with satisfaction, "I did it" feeling, validation of work
Physical: Standing tall, sighing with relief and satisfaction, displaying work, chest expanded, grounded stance

CONFIDENT

Description: Feeling sure of yourself and your abilities; trusting in your capacity to handle situations.
Triggers: Repeated success, positive feedback, preparation paying off, overcoming fears, mastery of skills, self-knowledge
Characteristics: Self-assurance, decisiveness, lack of self-doubt, comfortable taking risks, speaking up, assertiveness, inner certainty
Physical: Upright posture, steady eye contact, firm handshake, strong voice, relaxed but alert, taking up space comfortably

SUCCESSFUL

Description: Having achieved desired aims or attained prosperity; feeling that your efforts have produced positive results.
Triggers: Meeting goals, financial achievement, career advancement, winning, recognition of achievements, quantifiable results
Characteristics: Sense of effectiveness, validation from external results, momentum feeling, optimism about future, proof of capability
Physical: Upright posture, energized movements, bright expression, strong presence, victorious gestures (fist pump, arms raised)

EMPOWERED

Description: Feeling strong, capable, and in control of your life; having authority over your circumstances and choices.
Triggers: Making important decisions, standing up for yourself, breaking free from limitations, exercising agency, claiming your power
Characteristics: Sense of personal power, autonomy, self-determination, ability to influence outcomes, freedom from victimhood, agency
Physical: Strong posture, commanding presence, grounded stance, powerful movements, eye contact, taking up space, firm gestures
